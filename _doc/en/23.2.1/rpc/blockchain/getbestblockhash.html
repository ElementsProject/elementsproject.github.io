--- layout: doc name: getbestblockhash elemversion: 23.2.1 elemgroup: blockchain permalink: /en/doc/23.2.1/rpc/blockchain/getbestblockhash/ --- getbestblockhash Returns the hash of the best (tip) block in the most-work fully-validated chain.
