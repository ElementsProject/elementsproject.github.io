--- layout: doc name: dumpassetlabels elemversion: 0.18.1.5 elemgroup: util permalink: /en/doc/0.18.1.5/rpc/util/dumpassetlabels/ --- dumpassetlabels Lists all known asset id/label pairs in this wallet. This list can be modified with `-assetdir` configuration argument. Examples: > elements-cli dumpassetlabels > curl --user myusername --data-binary '{"jsonrpc": "1.0", "id":"curltest", "method": "dumpassetlabels", "params": [] }' -H 'content-type: text/plain;' http://127.0.0.1:7041/
