--- layout: doc name: dumpblindingkey elemversion: 0.18.1.7 elemgroup: wallet permalink: /en/doc/0.18.1.7/rpc/wallet/dumpblindingkey/ --- dumpblindingkey "address" Dumps the private blinding key for a CT address in hex. Arguments: 1. address (string, required) The CT address Result: "blindingkey" (string) The blinding key Examples: > elements-cli dumpblindingkey "my address"
